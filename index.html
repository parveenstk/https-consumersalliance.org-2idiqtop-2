How Can I Know If My Identity Has Been Stolen?

Signs to know if your identity has been stolen:

  • Unfamiliar charges on your bank or credit card accounts
  • You receive bills or credit offers for accounts you didn't open
  • You discover errors on your credit report
  • Credit or debit card is unexpectedly declined

How Do I Lock or ‚ÄúFreeze‚Äù My Credit?

You can lock your credit through any of the 3 credit bureaus: Experian, TransUnion, or Equifax. Another, even more secure way is through an identity theft protection company. Either way, a credit freeze makes it impossible for potential fraudsters to access while also being easy to unlock when needed.

What Is Identity Theft Insurance?

Identity theft insurance helps cover costs associated with restoring your identity if it's stolen. Also known as identity theft coverage, it can include anything from legal fees and lost wages to expenses for repairing your credit and reimbursement for stolen funds.
